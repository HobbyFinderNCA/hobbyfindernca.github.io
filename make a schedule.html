Hobby Finder: Make a Schedule

How to do it:

Type in already scheduled events and their times in the boxes (i.e.School 8:30-3:15 Rugby practice 5:00-6:00).Then, type in non-scheduled events in the boxes below the calender.If you want/need to do something more than once a month,type it in twice or more. It will make a calender for you.Please print before you leave this page.
